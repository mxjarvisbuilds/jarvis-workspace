Common questions

Everything you need to know before submitting.

Is this really free for homeowners?
Yes, 100% free. Contractors pay a fee only when they close a job. You never pay anything to use NeighborlyWork.
Will I get spammed with calls?
No. Your contact details are only shared with the contractor you choose. Contractors can ask clarifying questions through our secure chat before you decide, but they never get your phone number until you pick them.
How fast will I get quotes?
Most homeowners receive at least one quote within 24 hours. We target three quotes within 48 hours for all requests.
Are the contractors vetted?
Yes. We only work with licensed and insured HVAC contractors in the Sacramento, Roseville, and Folsom service area. No random handymen or out-of-area operators.
What if none of the quotes work for me?
No problem. There's no obligation. You're free to decline all quotes, ask for revisions, or simply walk away. You're in control.
What areas do you cover?
We currently serve Sacramento, Roseville, Folsom, Citrus Heights, Elk Grove, and surrounding communities. Expanding soon.

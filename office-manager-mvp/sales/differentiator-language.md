# Differentiator Language — HVAC Lead Recovery

## Core angle
This is not surveillance AI, call-recording theater, or another dashboard for a busy owner to ignore. It is a practical office-manager layer that recovers missed calls and stale estimates first, then keeps the same job record clean enough to hand off into dispatch, customer updates, and invoice-ready closeout.

## Positioning pillars
1. **Revenue first:** recover missed calls and stale estimates before building broader workflow.
2. **Human handoff:** urgent/high-value jobs go to a person fast; the system stays in its lane.
3. **Tech-friendly:** no surveillance-style coaching or nagging; keep field teams aligned without making them feel watched.
4. **One job record:** the same customer/job context can later carry through scheduling, ETA updates, photos, parts/time notes, and closeout.
5. **Owner relief:** reduce communication overload for thinly staffed shops without replacing their judgment.

## Copy blocks
- “Recover the jobs you already paid to earn — missed calls, forgotten estimates, and follow-ups that slip when the day gets chaotic.”
- “Not surveillance AI. Not another CRM replacement. A lightweight office-manager layer that keeps owner, office, and tech aligned.”
- “Start with the money leak: missed calls and stale estimates. Then carry the same job record into dispatch and closeout when you’re ready.”
- “Fast text-back, controlled follow-up, and human takeover for urgent jobs — so customers feel answered instead of automated.”

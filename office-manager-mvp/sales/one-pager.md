# Never Lose Another Missed Call Lead

## The promise
Stop losing jobs because nobody answered the phone or followed up on the estimate. This is not surveillance AI or another CRM replacement — it is a lightweight office-manager layer built to recover revenue first.

## What this does for you
- Catches missed-call leads before they disappear to the next contractor
- Follows up on estimates so money doesn’t die in your pipeline
- Protects your Google rating by filtering unhappy customers into private feedback first
- Keeps owner, office, and tech aligned without surveillance-style coaching or call-recording theater

## How it works
1. A customer calls and nobody answers
2. They get a text back from your business number right away
3. The system collects the basics and flags urgent or high-value jobs for human takeover
4. Qualified leads get pushed toward booking
5. Sent estimates get followed up automatically until they reply, book, or close out

# Service Agreement Template

> Plain-English template for contractor #1. Not legal advice. Have a real attorney review before using with a live client.

## Parties
This agreement is between:
- Provider: ____________________
- Client: ____________________

## Services Provided
Provider will build and manage the following Office Manager MVP services for Client:
- Missed call text-back
- AI lead intake
- Booking flow with calendar integration
- Estimate follow-up sequence (Day 1 / 3 / 7 / 14)
- Gated review request flow
- Human handoff for urgent/high-value leads
- SMS sent from Client’s business number
- Weekly performance summary text

The following are not included in v1 unless added separately in writing:
- Email marketing
- Web chat widget
- Existing customer database review campaigns
- CRM two-way sync
- Custom reporting/dashboard

## Pricing
- One-time setup fee: $199, due before build begins
- First month of service: free
- Monthly service fee after the free month: $497/month
- The first $497 monthly charge begins on Day 31 after launch

## Term
This agreement includes a 3-month minimum commitment period at $497/month after the free month ends.
After that minimum commitment period, this agreement continues month-to-month.
Either party may cancel after the minimum commitment period with 30 days’ written notice.

## Guarantee
If the system does not recover more revenue than it costs within 60 days of launch, Client may request a full refund of fees paid during that period.
This guarantee is an early-exit protection and overrides the remaining minimum commitment if validly invoked within that 60-day window.
If Client cancels under this guarantee, Provider will keep the system running for up to 30 additional days at no extra charge to allow for transition.

## Data Ownership
- Client owns all of its customer and lead data
- Provider owns the system structure, templates, workflows, prompts, and implementation method
- Upon cancellation, Client retains its customer data and contact history

## SMS Compliance
Client agrees to cooperate with all SMS compliance requirements, including A2P 10DLC registration, business verification, and any platform documentation needed to send compliant business text messages.
Client understands the system should not be used for unlawful, deceptive, or non-consensual messaging.

## Liability Limits
Provider is not responsible for lost profits, indirect damages, or consequential damages.
Provider’s total liability under this agreement will not exceed the total amount paid by Client under this agreement in the 60 days before the claim.

## Termination + Handoff
If this agreement ends:
- Client’s data remains Client’s
- Provider may remove or disable Provider-owned templates, automations, and system structure after the notice period or guarantee transition period ends
- Provider will give reasonable cooperation for orderly handoff

## Attorney Review Items
Have a real attorney review:
- guarantee wording
- refund language
- how the guarantee overrides the minimum commitment
- limitation of liability
- data ownership language
- cancellation/termination language
- SMS compliance acknowledgment

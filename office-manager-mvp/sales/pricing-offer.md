# Pricing + Offer

## Internal Version

### Offer
- Setup fee: $199 one-time, paid upfront before build begins
- First month: free
- Monthly fee after free month: $497/month
- Terms: 3-month minimum commitment at $497/month after the free month ends, then month-to-month with 30-day cancellation notice
- Guarantee: 60-day money-back guarantee if the system does not recover more revenue than it costs. If the contractor cancels under the guarantee, the system stays live for 30 additional days at no charge while they transition.

### Included
- Missed call text-back
- AI lead intake
- Booking flow with calendar integration
- Estimate follow-up sequence (Day 1 / 3 / 7 / 14)
- Gated review request
- Human handoff for urgent/high-value leads
- SMS sent from their business number
- Weekly performance summary text

### Not Included in v1
- Email marketing
- Web chat widget
- Existing customer database review campaigns
- CRM two-way sync
- Custom reporting/dashboard

### Reasoning
- $199 setup lowers the initial friction without making the offer feel disposable.
- A free first month gives the contractor a real proof window instead of just a pitch.
- The 3-month minimum after the free month protects against churn before results have time to compound.
- The guarantee still de-risks the decision while keeping the offer serious.

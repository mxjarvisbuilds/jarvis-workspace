# Target Contractor Criteria

## Best Contractor #1 Profile

### Trade Type Preference
1. HVAC
2. Plumbing
3. Electrical

### Why
These trades have:
- urgent inbound demand
- high missed-call value
- enough ticket size for ROI to be obvious
- recurring estimate leakage that follow-up can recover

## Company Size
### Best fit
- owner-operator or small team
- roughly 1-10 techs
- roughly 1-5 trucks
- enough lead flow that missed calls and stale estimates actually matter

### Too small
- solo operator with almost no inbound volume
- no scheduling pressure
- no existing estimate pipeline

### Too big
- larger operation with heavy internal process, call center layers, and longer procurement cycles

## Geographic Preference
- U.S.-based local service businesses
- metro or strong suburban markets
- places where fast response matters and homeowners comparison-shop quickly

## Good Fit Signals
- active website
- active Google Business Profile
- decent existing review base
- clearly accepts inbound calls/leads
- owner still close enough to revenue pain to care
- not already running a polished lead-response machine

## Red Flags
- anti-tech owner who resists even basic texting workflows
- chaotic business with no one willing to own setup inputs
- extremely low lead volume
- bargain-hunter energy / wants free custom work
- enterprise-level operation with too many approval layers

## Best Channels to Find Them
- Google Maps / local service search
- referrals
- local Facebook business groups
- BNI / local networking groups
- local contractor associations
- warm intros from marketing people already serving trades
